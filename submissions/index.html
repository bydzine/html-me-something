Summary:

Multimedia Design and Development (MDD) is the art of integrating multiple forms of media.

Education:

Education include learning to create web content, pages, marketing collateral, advertising, instructional material, and multimedia projects. MDD is the ability to integrate to or more of the following;

MDD is a form of communication that entails a detailed eye, ear and use of both sides of your brain. Creating the idea is only a small part of the job. You must be able to utilize technical programs to bring your project to life. Yes, you will work with technical professionals, however, you will still need to know and understand many programs that create animations, videos, websites, advertisements, print products, etc... Understanding the needs of your client is key. A MDD is a superhero of sorts. A producer of mixed minds, talents and observations.
